About Us

UNFOLD is the first undergraduate journal of English Language Education in Indonesia that unleashes potentials, fosters inquiries, and leverages dreams among youths and educators in Indonesia. We are based in Universitas Brawijaya, Malang, Indonesia. Currently in Indonesia, We identified three underlying urgent needs that obstruct undergraduate students from understanding how to improve our education quality, specifically in research which is:

  • Need of understanding (Benefits of conducting research)
  • Need of support group (Networking and ideas exchange among young researcher)
  • Need of publication (platform to submit and share the research)

with that, UNFOLD came as a publication platform dedicated to undergraduate research and creative endeavors related to English language Education in Indonesia.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm currently studying in master degree, can I submit my research in here?

Unfortunately, we only accept paper from undergraduate students. but you can submit your research paper into EDUCAFL. for more information about EDUCAFL, you can visit their website on the link.

How do I know my paper gets accepted or not?

We will send you the information status about your journal throughout your e-mail and your account. kindly check your e-mail and account regularly

Is there any charges or fee to submit my research paper?

you can submit your research paper without any charge or fee! it's completely FREE.

What happens if my paper gets rejected??

your paper may be rejected due to several reason such as research quality, administration or not following the paper template.

How do I submit my paper?

You can submit your paper by simply login into your account and submit it throughout submit my paper button. If you still do not have any account, you can create one!
